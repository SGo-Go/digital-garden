# Spain

## [Andalucía](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andalusia)

**Core Sights**:
- [**Granada**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Granada):
  [Alhambra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alhambra) [^Alhambra-Tickets],
  [tariterias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tapas)
- [**Nerja**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nerja):
  [Nerja Cave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caves_of_Nerja),
  [Balcón de Europa](https://www.tripadvisor.es/Attraction_Review-g315917-d2179640-Reviews-Balcony_of_Europe-Nerja_Costa_del_Sol_Province_of_Malaga_Andalucia.html)
- [**El Chorro**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Chorro):
  [Caminito del Rey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caminito_del_Rey) [^Caminito_del_Rey-Tickets]
- [**Tarifa**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tarifa):
  [Bolonia dunes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bolonia,_Spain) (with *ruins of Baelo City*),
  sunset over Morocco/[Gibraltar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strait_of_Gibraltar) at [Mirador África](https://www.tripadvisor.de/Attraction_Review-g315918-d5292807-Reviews-Mirador_del_Estrecho-Tarifa_Costa_de_la_Luz_Andalucia.html)
- [**Cadiz**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C%C3%A1diz):city center
- [**Sevilla**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seville)
- [**Fuengirola**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fuengirola)/[**Mijas**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mijas_Pueblo):
  [Bioparc](https://www.bioparcfuengirola.es/en/) (especially lemurs),
  Los Boliches, 
  [Mijas Puebla](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mijas_Pueblo) (sunset + museums)
- [**Ronda**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ronda)/[**Marbella**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marbella):
  [Puente Nuevo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puente_Nuevo)

[^Alhambra-Tickets]: Better purchase ticket in advance 
[^Caminito_del_Rey-Tickets]: Better purchase ticket in advance https://www.caminitodelrey.info/en/tickets/buy


**Cities/Towns by Activity**:
- tapas: Granada, Fuengirola
- surfing: Tarifa, Marbella, Fuengirola
- kyting & wind-surfing: Tarifa
- kayaking: Nerja
- beaches & nightlife: Nerja, Marbella, Fuengirola
